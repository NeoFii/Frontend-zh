## Funding Announcement

Eucal AI officially announces the completion of tens of millions in Series A funding, led by a renowned investment institution with participation from multiple strategic investors.

### Use of Funds

This round of funding will primarily be used for:

1. **Technology R&D**: Increase investment in core technologies like AI large model optimization and inference acceleration
2. **Team Expansion**: Attract top AI talent, expand R&D and sales teams
3. **Market Expansion**: Broaden market coverage to serve more enterprise customers
4. **Product Iteration**: Accelerate product iteration and launch more innovative features

### About Eucal AI

Founded in 2026, Eucal AI is an innovative technology company focused on AI technology R&D and applications. We are committed to transforming cutting-edge AI technology into practical business value, providing intelligent solutions for enterprises.

### Investor Comments

The lead investor stated: "Eucal AI's team has profound technical accumulation in the AI field, and their TierFlow product excels in performance and cost optimization. We believe Eucal AI will become an important force in the enterprise AI services sector."

### Future Plans

After completing this round of funding, Eucal AI will continue to deepen its presence in the enterprise AI services market, constantly improve product competitiveness, and create greater value for customers.
