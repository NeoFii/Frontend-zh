## Trend Analysis

With the widespread adoption of large language models, AI inference is becoming a key technology for enterprise digital transformation. Based on industry observations and data accumulation, Eucal AI provides insights into AI inference development trends for 2026.

### Trend 1: Cost Optimization as Core Priority

When enterprises adopt AI technology, cost control becomes the primary consideration. Intelligent caching, model routing, and other technologies will help enterprises reduce inference costs by over 70%.

### Trend 2: Rise of Edge Inference

With the development of edge computing technology, edge inference will play an important role in IoT, autonomous driving, and other fields. Eucal AI is deploying edge inference solutions.

### Trend 3: Multimodal Inference Integration

Fusion inference of text, image, voice, and other modalities will become mainstream. Eucal AI will continue investing in multimodal inference technology research.

### Trend 4: Industry-Specific Solutions

General inference services will evolve toward industry-specific solutions. Eucal AI will launch dedicated inference services for finance, healthcare, education, and other industries.

### Trend 5: Enhanced Security and Compliance Requirements

With the improvement of data security regulations, security and compliance requirements for AI inference services will continue to increase. Eucal AI will continuously strengthen security capabilities to provide reliable services for enterprises.
