## Enterprise Edition Release

To meet the diverse needs of large enterprises, Eucal AI officially launches the Enterprise Edition, providing more complete enterprise-level services.

### Core Features

- **Private Deployment**: Deploy on enterprise's own servers to ensure data security
- **Customized Models**: Custom inference models tailored to enterprise needs
- **Dedicated Support**: 7x24 professional technical support
- **SLA Guarantee**: Service level agreement to ensure quality of service

### Use Cases

Enterprise Edition is especially suitable for:

1. **Finance Industry**: Financial institutions with extremely high data security requirements
2. **Healthcare**: Medical institutions requiring sensitive medical data processing
3. **Government**: Government departments with strict data sovereignty requirements
4. **Large Enterprises**: Medium to large enterprises requiring customized services
